The Rubik's cube


The rubiks' cube is one of the world's most recognisable puzzles, having enjoyed hundreds of millions of sales since the little cube-shaped puzzles have first appeared in Hungarian toy stores. This six-sided puzzle has puzzled and captivated anyone who dared attempt them for the longest of times, and they remain a popular passtime as of today. This site aims to help you explore the world of this little magic cube.
